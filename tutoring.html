Project Overview

I started this organization in May 2021 with the goal of tutoring new-coming students from South Korea in all things STEM. As someone who moved to Canada at 5 years old, I knew how difficult the transition of moving countries was, especially with a language barrier (and this was just kindergarten!) After seeing so many young Korean students struggling within academics, knowing how intelligent and capable they actually were, I decided to take initiative. I wanted to make a space where students would not just get help with STEM subjects, but also have somewhere and someone who would understand to go to when things inevitably got difficult.

So, KS Tutoring started just 3 months after beginning the effort, with just myself and 5 students. Each day of the week I would meet with one student for an hour, helping with whatever subjects they needed, often incorperating half-hour english lessons, and sometimes just talking about their days. Seeing these students grow within the next year as both students and people was incredible and inspiring. Because of this I wanted to expand my outreach, so I brought in more tutors and more students as time went on. Now, KS Tutoring has 5 tutors and 20 students learning and growing with us!

This has been my passion project since I was 16, so thank you for taking the time to learn about us! I hope you enjoyed my long spiel about it :)
